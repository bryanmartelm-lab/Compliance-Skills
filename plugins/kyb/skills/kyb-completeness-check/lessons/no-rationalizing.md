# No rationalizing — follow the rulebook exactly

When applying KYB review logic, follow `SKILL.md` and `OUTPUT_SCHEMA.md` as written. Do not
rationalize, debate edge cases, or invent exceptions.

If the skill says a condition is a hard failure → it is a hard failure. Full stop.
If the skill says a document is required → it is required. Full stop.

The rules are sufficient. Rationalizing around them produces wrong outcomes.
